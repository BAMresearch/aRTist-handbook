Parameter Panel
===============

The parameter panel on the left-hand side of the aRTist main window (:numref:`guiMainWindow`) contains all the important controls for configuring the simulator. They are arranged on four tabs: **Setup**, **Source**, **Scattering**, and **Detector**.
